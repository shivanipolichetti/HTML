Laxman Narasimhan

Laxman Narasimhan is an Indian-American business executive, and the chief executive officer of Starbucks.He was chief commercial officer (CCO) of PepsiCo, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Reckitt. He joined Starbucks as interim CEO in October 2022, and succeeded Howard Schultz as CEO in April 2023.
